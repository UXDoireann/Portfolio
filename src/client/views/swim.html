Introduction

Open water swimming is a sport that has seen a 'rising tide' of popularity in Ireland in recent times. Even before the pandemic, the health benefits - both mental and physical - along with the camaraderie and love for the water had increased the numbers of enthusiasts taking the plunge. Lockdown restrictions saw an accelerated wave of new swimmers, with news of the sport's new found favour even making waves offshore.

However, for swimmers looking to find out important information before a swim eg. tide times, sea conditions, water temperature and more - it can be difficult. Tide and sea apps are mostly aimed at other 'sea-activities' eg. sailing, surfing or fishing. I wanted to design an app just for open water swimmers to help them find the information they needed before a swim, to help them getting the best of of their swim, while staying safe.

Goal

A passion project I'm designing, the goal is create an app for open water swimmers to check tide times and sea conditions for a desired swim spot.

Research

Online Survey

Firstly, I created a short online survey to get a broad initial sense of how open water swimmers use websites or apps to find out required information before a swim, what kind of information they look for and what problems they experience in finding that information. Sharing this survey on open water swim groups on social media, I quickly amassed 265 responses.

  • Most swimmers used mobile apps or websites before their swim to check tides, check weather and/or connect with a swim buddy.

  • Apps used to check tides include: Magic Seaweed; Tides Near Me; High Tide; Tide Times.

  • Swimmers used more than one app to find information eg. one for weather, another for tides, etc

  • Problems experienced using tide apps include: being difficult to navigate; information being confusing or having to to alot of searching and scrolling.
